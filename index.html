The Motivated Strategies for Learning Questionnaire (MSLQ) is an 81-question survey to assess your motivations and learning strategies towards schoolwork.

There are no right or wrong answers and this is not a test. For the best results, answer each question with your initial "gut" reaction - each question should take about 5-10 seconds to answer.
